Arceneaux Approach 2.0
Lateral stretch (15s x 2/3)
    - To the side
    - Palms forward
Leaning Stretch (10s each, 15s each, 30s each)
    - Horse stance
    - Palms forward
    - Lower hand hangs, upper reaches
    - Bend at waist
    - Don't hold breath
Yogi Breath (30s x2, 45s)
    - Good posture, sitting or standing
    - Inhale slowly throughout
    - After first sequence, repeat with hold at 60s.
